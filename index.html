My History

2016 - 2019

It all started off the coast of Morocco, on a ship where I served as a marine mechanical officer. I had some incredible experiences and came across the world of control systems that fascinated me deeply. I knew I wanted to specialize in this filed.

2019 - 2022

Here I am in France to become a control-command engineer. I was happy to uncover the secrets of these complex systems. Each step forward confirmed that I was on the right way but, my story was far from over

2022 - 2025

I joined a major French railway company, working on monitoring systems that once sparked my passion. it was a fulfilling experience blending innovation & precision. Yet, over time, I felt a missing piece: Creativity. And that's when a new idea started to cross my mind...

2025 - Beyond

On March 14, 2025, I made a bold decision: to venture into the the world of Web developpement as a self taught learner. I saw it as the perfect blend of technical & creativity, a space where I could build & innovate freely. I am now committed to furthering my skills through a formal training program, with the goal of mastering this profession and providing meaningful digital solutions to others.
